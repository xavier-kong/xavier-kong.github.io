About Me

Like most people, I started learning coding through Python during the summer of 2020. However, I quickly lost interest due to the lack of interesting projects that could be completed by a beginner. During the summer of 2021, inpsired by the team I was interning with at the time, I tried coding again. This time starting with the basics of HTML, CSS and Javascript. I fell in love building simple websites and decided to take a course offered by the University of Helsinki called Full Stack Open 2021. With this course I learnt the basics of full stack development with the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React.js, Node.js) as well testing with Cypress and Jest. Since then I've learnt a lot more but I am always hungry for more knowledge.
